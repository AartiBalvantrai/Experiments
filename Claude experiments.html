About

I hold an audience the way radio taught me to — with voice, timing, and a story worth staying for.

Across Radio Mirchi, Radio One and Radio City I produced 700+ hours of original content, hosted prime-time to 300,000 listeners, and interviewed 500+ guests — from the Indian Prime Minister to Bollywood's biggest names. I built IPs like Tech Talk and Radio for Change, and helped the Election Commission move voters in Gujarat.

Then I brought that instinct online. At Criya (a San Francisco YC startup) I co-built content and community IP for founders; at upliance.ai I lead AI content and influencer strategy — growing Instagram 4× in four months and lifting YouTube CTR 28% — while shaping a new multi-product brand. Sharp enough to convert, human enough to remember.
